## THE TWO PRODUCTS

### Product 1: Growmax Revenue Platform (Enterprise)
- **Target:** Large manufacturers (like Siemens, Schwing Stetter, OBO Bettermann) who need dealer portals, partner commerce, spares portals, sales rep quoting, and customer self-service ordering
- **Buyer persona:** VP of Sales, VP of Channel Operations, CTO, Digital Transformation Lead at industrial manufacturers with 500+ employees
- **Sales motion:** Consultative, demo-driven, 2-6 month sales cycle
- **Pricing:** Custom/enterprise (not displayed publicly — "Contact Sales")
- **Key differentiators:** SAP/ERP integration, multi-tenant architecture, go-live in weeks not months, proven with global industrial brands
- **CTA:** "Book a Demo" / "Talk to Sales"
- **Existing reference:** Current growmax.io/b2b-commerce-cloud page

### Product 2: Growmax ARC (SMB Distributors)
- **Target:** Small-to-mid FMCG distributors and wholesalers globally (5-50 employees) — food & beverage, electrical, consumer goods
- **Buyer persona:** Owner/founder, operations manager of a distribution company currently running on WhatsApp + Excel + phone calls
- **Sales motion:** Product-led growth, self-serve signup, instant value
- **Pricing:** $199/month for 5 users (displayed prominently)
- **What's included:** Inventory management + Sales rep mobile app + B2B customer self-ordering portal
- **Key differentiators:** Set up in minutes, no IT team needed, affordable, mobile-first, Zoho integrated
- **CTA:** "Start Free Trial" / "Sign Up Now"
- **Competitors being displaced:** B2B Wave, Pepperi, SimplyDepo, OrderCircle

## CRITICAL DESIGN PRINCIPLES

1. **Growmax Red (#D42B2B) is the hero brand color.** It must appear on EVERY page as the primary CTA button color. The red interlocking knot icon is the most recognizable brand element — use it consistently.

2. **The two products must feel distinctly different.** Enterprise = dark navy backgrounds + red accents + authority. ARC = white/warm backgrounds + red CTAs + simplicity.

3. **The homepage is a router.** Its #1 job is to get visitors to the right product within 5 seconds.

4. **Preserve SEO equity.** The existing site has indexed blog posts and solution pages. Every old URL must have a working 301 redirect.

5. **Mobile-first for ARC.** Desktop-first for Revenue Platform. Both must work on both.

6. **The Shell reference is important.** The existing site mentions "Trusted by Shell, Growmax (Amshuhu) has transformed operations for over 300 distributors across 50+ countries." Use this proof point.

7. **No stock photos of people in suits.** Prefer: warehouses, distribution centers, tablets/phones showing apps, product packaging on shelves, factory floors.

8. **Every page loads fast.** No hero videos on initial load. Static images or CSS/SVG animations for visual interest. The site should work perfectly without JavaScript (SSR all critical content).
